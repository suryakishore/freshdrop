# FreshDrop Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** 23 July 2026 Welcome to **FreshDrop**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FreshDrop collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. --- # 1. Information We Collect ## Personal Information When you register or use FreshDrop, we may collect: * Name * Mobile Number * Delivery Address * Device Information * Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) Token * Session ID (used for secure login) --- ## Location Information FreshDrop collects your delivery location only to: * Deliver orders accurately * Check whether delivery is available in your area * Improve delivery experience We do not continuously track your location. --- ## Order Information When you place an order we store: * Ordered products * Quantity * Vendor * Delivery Slot * Payment Method * Order Status * Delivery Address --- ## Vendor Information For vendor accounts we collect: * Vendor Name * Mobile Number * Service Area * Product Catalog * UPI ID (if provided) * Business Status (Online/Offline) --- # 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: * Create your account * Process orders * Connect customers with vendors * Send order notifications * Send payment updates * Improve app performance * Prevent fraudulent activity * Maintain account security --- # 3. Push Notifications FreshDrop uses **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** to send: * Order Updates * Vendor Notifications * Delivery Status * Payment Confirmation * Important Service Messages Notification permissions can be disabled from your device settings. --- # 4. Login Sessions FreshDrop uses secure session management. For security purposes: * Logging into another device may automatically log out previous sessions. * This helps protect your account from unauthorized access. --- # 5. Data Sharing FreshDrop **does not sell your personal information**. Your information is shared only: * Between customer and assigned vendor for order fulfillment. * With Firebase services required to operate the application. --- # 6. Third-Party Services FreshDrop uses trusted third-party services including: * Firebase Authentication * Firebase Realtime Database * Firebase Cloud Messaging These services have their own privacy policies. --- # 7. Data Security We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. While no online system is completely secure, we continuously work to safeguard your data. --- # 8. Data Retention We retain your information only as long as necessary to: * Complete orders * Maintain account history * Meet legal obligations * Improve service quality --- # 9. Children's Privacy FreshDrop is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. --- # 10. Your Rights You may: * Update your profile information * Change your delivery address * Log out from your account * Request deletion of your account by contacting us --- # 11. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date. --- # 12. Contact Us If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the FreshDrop application. --- © 2026 FreshDrop. All Rights Reserved.
