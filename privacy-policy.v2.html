FreshDrop Privacy Policy

Fresh Essentials. Delivered Daily.
Effective Date: 23 July 2026

1. Introduction

FreshDrop respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information while using the FreshDrop mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Delivery Address
  • Location (only for delivery purposes)
  • Order History
  • Device Notification Token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver your orders.
  • Notify vendors about new orders.
  • Send order status updates.
  • Improve our services.
  • Provide customer support.

4. Location Usage

FreshDrop accesses your location only to determine your delivery address and nearby service providers. Your location is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Notifications

FreshDrop uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send important notifications such as:

  • Order Accepted
  • Order Rejected
  • Delivery Updates
  • Payment Confirmation

6. Data Security

We use Firebase services to securely store application data. Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect user information.

7. Third-Party Services

FreshDrop currently uses:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging

8. Children's Privacy

FreshDrop is not intended for children under the age of 18.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
